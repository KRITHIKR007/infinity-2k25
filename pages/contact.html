Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

You can register for any event through our online registration portal. Simply go to the specific event page and click the "Register Now" button.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as there are no scheduling conflicts. Each event requires separate registration.

Is accommodation provided for outstation participants?

Limited accommodation is available on a first-come, first-served basis for outstation participants. Please contact us in advance to arrange accommodation.

What are the payment methods available?

We accept online payments through UPI, net banking, and credit/debit cards. Some events also allow cash payment at the venue.
