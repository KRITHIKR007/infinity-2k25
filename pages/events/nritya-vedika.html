Nritya Vedika

A group dance competition that brings teams together to perform synchronized and energetic routines in Indo-Western styles.

Event Overview

Nritya Vedika is a premier group dance competition that brings together teams to showcase their choreography, synchronization, and creativity through Indo-Western dance styles. This platform allows teams to combine traditional and contemporary dance forms to create mesmerizing performances.

The competition celebrates teamwork, coordination, and artistic expression as teams deliver powerful and synchronized routines. Whether it's the grace of classical Indian dance or the energy of contemporary styles, Nritya Vedika is your chance to make a statement on stage!

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team Size: 6-15 members per team.
  • Dance Form: Indo-Western styles.
  • Time Limit: 6-8 minutes per performance.
  • Props: Props are allowed but should be safe to use.
  • Audio Track: Final audio track must be submitted one day before the event.
  • Judging Criteria: Synchronization, Creativity, Choreography, and Stage Presence.
  • Costumes & Song Selection: Must be appropriate and adhere to cultural guidelines.
  • Teams must arrive at the venue at least 45 minutes before their scheduled performance.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
