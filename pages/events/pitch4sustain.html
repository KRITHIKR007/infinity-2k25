Sustainable Innovation Challenge

Venue

Room 002

Team Size

1-5 members

Registration Fee

₹500 per team

Event Description

PITCH4SUSTAIN challenges teams to develop innovative sustainability solutions that address key UN Sustainable Development Goals. Teams will create functional prototypes and pitch their ideas to a panel of judges.

Organized by $echo, Anova, GDG on Campus, and Zigbee clubs, this event offers participants the opportunity to showcase their creativity, technical skills, and commitment to sustainable development.

Rules & Regulations

  • Team Composition: 1-5 members with diverse skills in problem-solving, design, prototyping, and presentation.
  • Prototype Requirement: A functional prototype (hardware, software, simulation, or interactive demo) is mandatory.
  • Problem Statements: Your solution must address one of the following UN Sustainable Development Goals:
    • Good Health and Well-being
    • Decent Work and Economic Growth
    • Zero Hunger
    • Affordable and Clean Energy
    • Sustainable Cities and Communities
  • Presentation Guidelines: 10-minute pitch + 5-minute Q&A. Should include problem statement, technical aspects, sustainability benefits, demo, and implementation strategy.

Judging Criteria

Innovation (25%)

Originality and creativity of the solution

Technical Implementation (25%)

Quality and functionality of the prototype

Sustainability Impact (25%)

Potential to address chosen SDG

Presentation (25%)

Clarity, persuasiveness, and Q&A responses
