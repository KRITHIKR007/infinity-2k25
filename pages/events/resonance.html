Event Description

Resonance is a premier solo singing competition that celebrates vocal talent across a wide range of genres and styles. From classical and folk to contemporary and pop, this platform showcases the power and beauty of the human voice.

Whether you're classically trained or self-taught, this competition offers a prestigious stage to demonstrate your vocal range, technique, emotional expression, and stage presence. Captivate the audience with your unique vocal style and interpretation.

Song Categories

Classical

Indian classical traditions including Hindustani, Carnatic, or Western classical pieces. Demonstrates technical proficiency, raga/scale knowledge, and traditional vocal techniques.

Khayal Dhrupad Thumri Varnam Kriti Opera

Bollywood/Film

Popular songs from Hindi cinema or regional films. Focuses on expressive interpretation, capturing the song's essence, and connecting with the audience through familiar melodies.

Romantic Peppy Classical Folk-Inspired Sufi

Western

International music including pop, rock, R&B, jazz, country, and more. Evaluates vocal range, contemporary techniques, and stylistic authenticity.

Pop Rock Jazz R&B Country

Original Composition

Self-composed songs in any language or style. Judges songwriting ability, lyrical content, and musical creativity in addition to vocal performance.

Singer-Songwriter Indie Experimental Modern Folk

Rules & Guidelines

  • Performance Duration: Each participant will have 3-5 minutes for their performance. Time limits will be strictly enforced.
  • Accompaniment: Participants may use:
    • Pre-recorded karaoke/instrumental tracks (must be submitted 2 days prior)
    • Live accompaniment with a maximum of 2 instrumentalists (must be registered in advance)
    • Sing without accompaniment (a cappella)
  • Language: Songs can be performed in any language. For non-Hindi/English songs, participants should provide a brief translation/summary.
  • Equipment: Microphones and basic sound system will be provided. Participants must specify technical requirements during registration.
  • Selection Process: Participants must indicate their song choice and category during registration. Changes after submission will not be accepted.
  • Attire: Participants should dress appropriately for a formal competition. Presentable attire that complements the performance is encouraged.
  • Content Guidelines: Songs should be appropriate for all audiences. Material with explicit content or offensive language will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

Vocal Technique (30%) - Pitch accuracy, breath control, voice quality, and range

Expression & Emotion (25%) - Conveying the song's message and emotional content

Musicality (20%) - Rhythm, phrasing, dynamics, and stylistic interpretation

Stage Presence (15%) - Confidence, audience engagement, and overall performance

Difficulty & Originality (10%) - Song choice complexity and creative interpretation

Competition Format

1

Preliminary Round

All registered participants will perform one song of their choice. The judges will select the top 15 performers to advance to the semi-finals.

2

Semi-Final Round

The 15 semi-finalists will perform a different song from their preliminary performance. Participants may choose a song from the same or a different category. The top 5 will advance to the finals.

3

Final Round

The 5 finalists will perform a third song of their choice, different from their previous performances. This round will determine the winners based on the judging criteria.
