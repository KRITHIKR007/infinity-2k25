Event Description

Art Attack is a vibrant platform for visual artists to showcase their creative prowess across various traditional art forms. This competition celebrates the rich heritage of fine arts while encouraging artists to push boundaries and express their unique perspectives.

From the precision of sketching to the vibrancy of painting and the intricacy of mixed media creations, Art Attack invites participants to bring their artistic vision to life. Whether you're an established artist or an emerging talent, this is your opportunity to make a mark in the art world.

Competition Categories

Sketching & Drawing

Create detailed pencil, charcoal, or pen illustrations that demonstrate line work, shading, and composition. Subjects can range from portraiture and still life to abstract concepts.

Graphite Charcoal Ink Colored Pencil

Painting

Express your artistic vision through watercolors, acrylics, oils, or gouache. Focus on color theory, brush technique, and emotional impact in your compositions.

Watercolor Acrylic Oil Gouache

Mixed Media & Collage

Combine various materials, textures, and techniques to create multi-dimensional artwork. This category rewards experimentation, innovative use of materials, and conceptual thinking.

Paper Art Textile Found Objects 3D Elements

Traditional Indian Art

Celebrate India's rich artistic heritage through traditional art forms. Demonstrate knowledge of cultural symbolism, traditional techniques, and authentic representation.

Madhubani Warli Kalamkari Miniature Tanjore

Theme

"Harmony in Contrast: Tradition Meets Innovation"

"Where the threads of heritage intertwine with the strands of progress, where ancient wisdom converges with futuristic vision - create art that captures this delicate balance."

This theme invites artists to explore the dynamic relationship between traditional values and contemporary innovation. Create artwork that reflects how cultural heritage shapes modern identity, or how technological advances influence traditional practices.

Artists may interpret this theme literally or metaphorically across any competition category. The most compelling works will demonstrate thoughtful engagement with both traditional techniques and innovative approaches, crafting a visual narrative that bridges past and present.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Materials & Dimensions: Participants must bring their own materials. Artwork size must be between A3 (297 Ã— 420 mm) and A1 (594 Ã— 841 mm).
  • Time Constraints: The competition will last for 4 hours. All artwork must be completed within this timeframe.
  • Originality: All artwork must be original and created entirely during the competition. References may be brought but direct copying is prohibited.
  • Theme Interpretation: Artwork must address the competition theme "Harmony in Contrast: Tradition Meets Innovation" in some manner.
  • Category Selection: Participants must register for one specific category but may incorporate elements from other categories as appropriate.
  • Submission: Upon completion, artwork must be submitted with a title and a brief statement (50-100 words) explaining the concept and how it relates to the theme.
  • Conduct: Participants must maintain a respectful environment. Disturbing other competitors or creating offensive content will result in disqualification.
  • Copyright: Artists retain copyright of their work but grant Infinity 2025 non-exclusive rights to display and promote the submitted artwork with appropriate credit.

Judging Criteria

Technical Skill (30%) - Proficiency in chosen medium, execution quality, attention to detail

Creativity (25%) - Originality of concept, innovative approach, unique personal style

Composition (20%) - Visual balance, use of space, color harmony, effective design principles

Theme Interpretation (15%) - Meaningful connection to theme, depth of concept

Overall Impact (10%) - Emotional response, memorability, audience engagement
