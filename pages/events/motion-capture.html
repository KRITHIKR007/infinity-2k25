Photography Competition

Motion Capture

A photography competition exploring the theme of movement, energy, and dynamic moments.

Register Now Back to Events

Event Overview

Motion Capture is a photography competition that challenges participants to freeze dynamic moments in time. The competition focuses on capturing movement, energy, and action through still photography, showcasing the art of timing and composition.

From sports action to dance performances, flowing water to urban hustle, this event invites photographers to explore various interpretations of motion and movement through their lens. Show us how you can tell a story of motion in a single frame!

Rules & Guidelines

  • Theme: Motion and Movement
  • Submission: Each participant can submit up to 3 photographs.
  • Format: JPEG or PNG format, minimum resolution of 2000x1500 pixels.
  • Equipment: Any camera type is allowed (DSLR, mirrorless, smartphone, etc.).
  • Editing: Basic editing (brightness, contrast, cropping) is allowed, but heavy manipulation or composites are not permitted.
  • Originality: All photographs must be original work taken by the participant.
  • Ethics: Photos must not be offensive, harmful, or violate anyone's privacy rights.
  • Judging Criteria: Relevance to theme, technical quality, composition, creativity, and emotional impact.
  • Submission Deadline: All entries must be submitted three days before the event.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Tips for Participants

  • Experiment with shutter speeds to capture different aspects of motion (blur vs. freeze).
  • Consider panning techniques to keep your subject sharp while blurring the background.
  • Think beyond literal movement—consider implied motion through composition and subject selection.
  • Pay attention to lighting conditions, as good light can dramatically improve motion photography.
  • Look for unconventional subjects or moments that convey motion in unexpected ways.
