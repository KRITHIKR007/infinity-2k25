Event Description

Innovathon is a 24-hour innovation marathon where teams work to develop solutions to real-world challenges. From ideation to prototype, participants will experience the full cycle of product development compressed into a single day.

This high-energy event brings together programmers, designers, engineers, and business minds to collaborate on innovative projects across various domains including healthcare, education, environment, and technology.
