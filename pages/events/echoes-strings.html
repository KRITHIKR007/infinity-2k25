Event Description

Echoes & Strings is a live music competition that celebrates musical talent across genres. From rock and pop to indie, fusion, and classical, this platform welcomes diverse musical expressions featuring vocals and instrumental performances.

Whether you're a band, an acoustic duo, or a vocal ensemble, this is your opportunity to showcase your musical prowess, original compositions, and creative interpretations of existing songs. Let your music resonate with the audience and leave a lasting impression.

Competition Categories

Vocal Performance

Showcasing singing talent through solo or group vocal performances, with or without instrumental accompaniment. Focuses on vocal technique, expression, and interpretation.

Solo Vocals Duets A Cappella Choirs

Instrumental Performance

Focusing on musical skill through purely instrumental performances. Can include any genre and combination of instruments, emphasizing technical proficiency and musical composition.

Solo Instruments Ensembles Orchestral Fusion

Band Performance

Full band setups with various instruments and vocals. Evaluates collective musical cohesion, arrangement, and overall performance energy.

Rock Pop Metal Jazz Indie

Original Composition

Dedicated to self-composed music. Judges original songwriting, lyrical content, and musical innovation rather than just performance technique.

Original Songs Compositions Songwriting

Rules & Guidelines

  • Time Limit: Each performance must be between 5-10 minutes. This includes setup time. Exceeding the time limit will result in point deductions.
  • Equipment: Basic equipment including microphones, amplifiers, drum set, keyboard, and speakers will be provided. Participants must bring their own instruments (guitars, bass, specialized percussion instruments, etc.)
  • Performance Material: Participants can perform covers or original compositions. For covers, creative interpretation is encouraged rather than exact replication.
  • Backing Tracks: Pre-recorded backing tracks are allowed only for solo performances. These should be submitted in MP3 format at least two days before the event.
  • Registration Information: Teams must provide full details of all members, instruments played, and technical requirements during registration.
  • Soundcheck: A brief soundcheck will be provided to each team before their performance. The schedule will be shared on the day of the event.
  • Content Guidelines: Performances should be appropriate for all audiences. Songs with explicit content or offensive language may result in disqualification.

Judging Criteria

Musical Proficiency (30%) - Technical skill, instrument/vocal mastery, and execution

Expression (20%) - Emotional conveyance, dynamics, and interpretation

Creativity (20%) - Originality, arrangement, and innovation

Ensemble Cohesion (15%) - Synchronization and harmony among performers

Stage Presence (15%) - Audience engagement and overall performance energy

Competition Format

1

Preliminary Round

All registered teams will perform one song of their choice. Judges will select finalists from each category based on the judging criteria.

2

Final Round

Finalists will perform a different song from their preliminary performance. Winners will be selected from each category, and an overall champion will be chosen across all categories.
