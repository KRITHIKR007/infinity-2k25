DRONE XTREME

A precision drone challenge where teams navigate through an obstacle course and accurately deploy payloads.

Event Overview

DRONE XTREME is an exciting drone payload challenge that tests participants' piloting skills, precision, and strategic thinking. Teams will navigate custom-built drones through an obstacle course and deploy payloads at specific target zones with maximum accuracy.

This competition simulates real-world applications of drone technology such as disaster relief, package delivery, and environmental monitoring. Participants will gain hands-on experience with drone operations while competing in a challenging and dynamic environment.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team Size: 2-3 members per team.
  • Competition Format: Each team will navigate their drone through an obstacle course with multiple checkpoints and payload drop zones.
  • Drone Specifications:
  • Maximum drone size: 500mm diagonal motor-to-motor measurement
  • Maximum weight (including payload mechanism): 2kg
  • Must have a functional payload release mechanism
  • Course Elements:
  • Navigation through gates, hoops, and slalom markers
  • Multiple target zones of varying difficulties and point values
  • Time-based challenges requiring quick decision making
  • Scoring:
  • Course completion points: 50 points for successfully navigating the full course
  • Payload accuracy: 10-30 points per successful delivery, depending on target difficulty
  • Time bonus: Bonus points for completing within the optimal time window
  • Penalties: -5 points for each boundary violation or obstacle collision
  • Safety Regulations:
  • All drones must undergo a pre-flight safety inspection
  • Drones must have a functional fail-safe mechanism
  • Flight area will be netted for safety
  • Teams may bring their own drones or request to use provided drones (limited availability).
  • Teams will get two attempt runs, with the better score counting for final rankings.
