STEP SYNC

Solo Dance Competition

Venue

Colosseum

Participation

Individual

Registration Fee

₹500

Event Description

Step Sync is an electrifying solo dance competition that invites dancers to showcase their talent, creativity, and stage presence. Whether you specialize in Indian, Bollywood, Western, or Fusion styles, this is your chance to leave the audience mesmerized with your performance.

Express yourself through movement and rhythm as you compete against other talented performers for exciting prizes and the title of Step Sync Champion at Infinity 2025.

Rules & Regulations

  • Dance Styles: Indian, Bollywood, Western, or Fusion styles are allowed.
  • Time Limit: Each performance must be between 3-4 minutes. Exceeding the time limit will result in point deductions.
  • Audio Track: Participants must submit their final audio track one day before the event in MP3 format. Name your file with your registration number and name.
  • Props: Props are allowed but must be arranged by the participant. The setup and removal of props should be completed within 30 seconds.
  • Attire: Appropriate attire matching the theme of your performance is required. Provocative or offensive costumes are not permitted.
  • Reporting Time: Participants must report at least 1 hour before the scheduled performance time.
  • General Rules: Vulgarity in any form will lead to immediate disqualification. The judges' decision will be final and binding.

Judging Criteria

Choreography (25%)

Creativity, complexity, and structure of the routine

Technical Execution (25%)

Precision, coordination, and skill level

Expression & Musicality (20%)

Emotional connection and interpretation of music

Stage Presence (15%)

Confidence, energy, and audience engagement

Costume & Presentation (10%)

Appropriateness and creativity of attire

Overall Impact (5%)

Memorable and unique qualities of the performance

Competition Format

1

Preliminary Round

All registered participants will perform their complete routine. The top 10 performers will advance to the final round.

2

Final Round

The finalists will perform again, potentially with some modifications to their routine. Winners will be selected based on the judges' scores.
