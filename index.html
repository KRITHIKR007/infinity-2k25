About INFINITY-2K25

INFINITY-2K25 is a premier national-level techno-cultural college fest organized by Jain University in Bangalore. Join us for an unforgettable experience of innovation, competition, and networking on March 27-28, 2025.

This year's theme "Past to Future" celebrates the fusion of tradition and innovation, bringing together the brightest minds from across the country.

Since 2018, INFINITY has grown to become one of South India's leading college festivals, attracting talented participants from over 100 colleges nationwide. Our mission is to create a vibrant platform where students showcase their skills, exchange ideas, and build lasting connections.
