About INFINITY-2K25

INFINITY-2K25 is the annual national-level technical and cultural fest of Faculty of Engineering and Technology, Jain (Deemed-to-be University), Bangalore. It provides a platform for students to showcase their technical and creative skills, connect with industry experts, and compete for exciting prizes.

Technical Innovation

Explore cutting-edge technology through competitions, workshops, and tech talks designed to enhance your technical skills.

Cultural Showcase

Celebrate creativity and talent through various cultural events including dance, music, art, and drama.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with industry leaders, professionals, and peers to expand your network and explore new opportunities.
