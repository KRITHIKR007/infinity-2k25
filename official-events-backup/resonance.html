Resonance

Event Overview

Resonance is a dynamic singing competition designed for duets and groups to showcase their musical talent, harmony, and stage presence. This event celebrates the power of collaborative music through live performances spanning various genres and styles.

Whether you're a duo with perfect harmonies or a larger ensemble with complex vocal arrangements, Resonance provides the perfect stage to demonstrate your musical chemistry. From classical arrangements to contemporary hits, show us how your voices blend to create something magical.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Time Limit: 5 minutes for duets, 7 minutes for groups.
  • Background Music: No background music is allowed.
  • Instruments: Groups can use up to 2 instruments for accompaniment.
  • Team Size: 2 members for duets, 3-6 members for groups.
  • Judging Criteria: Harmony, vocal technique, synchronization, expression, and overall impact.
  • Participants must arrive at the venue at least 30 minutes before their scheduled performance time.
  • Lyrics should be appropriate and not contain offensive content.
  • Participants should inform organizers about any specific equipment requirements in advance.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
